# driver-alert-app
Every year, driver fatigue contributes to countless accidents and preventable tragedies. Driver Alert Monitor was built to make advanced drowsiness detection technology accessible to everyone — not just those with expensive vehicles or commercial safety systems.
The app uses your smartphone's front camera and on-device AI to monitor signs of driver fatigue and immediately trigger an audible alert when drowsiness is detected.
